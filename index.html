Overall Purpose &
Aims
of the Workshop

This workshop will provide educators with an opportunity to design units and create learning environments that connect cognitive and constructivist learning theories using real-world assessment practices. Teachers will engage with each section of the Middle Years Programme (MYP) unit planner in order to evaluate and reflect on current practices. As they build authentic units, teachers will discover the value of student-centered inquiry and a conceptually-driven curriculum. With a greater understanding of how to implement inquiry-based teaching and learning practices, teachers can foster the development of skills and knowledge their students will need to be active citizens in a 21st-century global community.


The aims of the teaching and learning of this workshop are to encourage and enable participants to:
● Recognize how creating authentic units informs meaningful teaching and learning
● Identify a variety of inclusive learning engagements that will support the creation of authentic units
● Evaluate how the design of authentic units engages students in relevant, real-world inquiry
● Reflect on the local and global impact of authentic teaching and learning.
