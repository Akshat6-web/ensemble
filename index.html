Ensemble

Ensemble is a well-thought initiative to bring all ib educators of the world in close proximity working across all the programmes of IB. Ensembles believe in its motto of “Becoming better together” and welcomes of IB educators to share their experiences, work, thoughts with their own names. Ensemble gives appropriate credit to the contributor. Ensemble believes in building student-centric approaches for teaching & learning. It also acts as a sharing platform and believes in giving educators an opportunity to relive their stress by penning down their thoughts on the blogs/vlogs at ensemble.
