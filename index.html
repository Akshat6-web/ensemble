Bringing Educators Together

Ensemble is a well-thought initiative to bring different perspectives on a common platform. This platform can be used by educators(IB & Non-IB)/parents/counselors/experts to share their work/thoughts/ideas /experiences on a global platform with due credit for their creativity.

Learning through sharing & experiences

Ensemble stands on its motto of becoming better together hence welcome all to share their experiences in the form of blogs/vlogs/thoughts/resources etc. from different fields

Scaffolding the educational content

Ensemble broadens the horizon by scaffolding different elements in the education field with respect to national (State/CBSE/ICSE) & international curriculum (IB/Cambridge) which is useful for Educator & Parents.
