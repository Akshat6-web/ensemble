About us

Ensemble is a networking platform. It is a well-thought initiative with the motto of “ Becoming Better together”. We are dedicated to create ideas that create more ideas.

Ensemble

Ensemble is a well-thought initiative to bring all ib educators of the world in close proximity working across all the programmes of IB. Ensembles believe in its motto of “Becoming better together” and welcomes of IB educators to share their experiences, work, thoughts with their own names. Ensemble gives appropriate credit to the contributor. Ensemble believes in building student-centric approaches for teaching & learning. It also acts as a sharing platform and believes in giving educators an opportunity to relive their stress by penning down their thoughts on the blogs/vlogs at ensemble.
Disclaimer: This is a noncommercial website and intends for sharing resources/information/thoughts only, no monetary benefit associated with this.

About the founder

Ms Sheeza is the initiator for a well thought networking platform “ Ensemble” which works on the motto of becoming better together. Ms. Sheeza is a proponent of active learning which brings good to all and values experiential education and hence invites experts as contributors to share their knowledge, experience thoughts & ideas at Ensemble.She is open minded, optimistic & believe in sharing knowledge. Being an educator she is of the strong opinion that education is meaningful if it is able to instill the combination of knowledge and skills to create lifelong internationally minded learners. Ms. Sheeza is associated with the field of education since 2007 and has a vast experience in various educational boards from Montessori to CBSE, ICSE, IGCSE, and IB. She is an active practitioner of IB since almost 12 years now and throughout her IB journey, she has worked in various capacities across three programmes - IB PYP, IB MYP and IB DP. She is an IB Examiner and possesses experience for MYP French as Language Acquisition, MYP Personal Project in two languages (French and English) and DP for French ab-initio and B SL. She is also associated with esteemed International Baccalaureate educator network (IBEN)as a Workshop Leader & as a School Visit Team Member and works for the global quality assurance and consistency in the delivery of IB services around the world.
